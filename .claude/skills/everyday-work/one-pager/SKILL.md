# One-Pager Generator

Creates a single-page brief for any program, initiative, event, or proposal. Designed for leadership consumption — clear, scannable, decision-ready.

## Trigger Phrases

- `/one-pager`
- `/one-pager "NFL Hackathon"`
- `create a one-pager for [topic]`
- `give me a brief on [topic]`
- `I need a one-pager for leadership`

## One-Pager Template

```markdown
# [Program/Initiative Name]
**Prepared for:** [Audience] | **Date:** [Date] | **Owner:** [Name]

---

## What
[2-3 sentences describing what this is. Be specific.]

## Why
[2-3 sentences on why this matters. Tie to business goals, OKRs, or strategic priorities.
Include a competitive angle if relevant.]

## Who
| Role | Name/Team |
|------|-----------|
| Owner | [name] |
| Executive sponsor | [name] |
| Key partners | [names/orgs] |
| Target audience | [description + size] |

## When
| Milestone | Date |
|-----------|------|
| [Milestone 1] | [Date] |
| [Milestone 2] | [Date] |
| [Launch/Event date] | [Date] |

## Key Metrics
| Metric | Target | Current |
|--------|--------|---------|
| [Metric 1] | [target] | [current or N/A] |
| [Metric 2] | [target] | [current or N/A] |

## Investment
| Item | Amount | Status |
|------|--------|--------|
| [Budget item] | [$ amount] | [Approved/Pending/Requested] |
| Total | [$ total] | |

## Status
**Overall:** [Green / Yellow / Red]
- [Key accomplishment or milestone hit]
- [Key risk or blocker if any]

## Ask
[What you need from the reader — approval, budget, decision, awareness. Be specific.]

---
*[Contact name] | [Contact info] | [Date]*
```

## Section Guidelines

| Section | Must Have | Avoid |
|---------|----------|-------|
| **What** | Specifics. "A 3-day virtual hackathon for 500+ Azure developers" | Vague descriptions. "An exciting new program" |
| **Why** | Business impact with numbers. "Drives 10K marketplace agent registrations" | Aspirational claims without evidence |
| **Who** | Named individuals and specific audience sizes | "Various stakeholders" |
| **When** | Hard dates for key milestones | "TBD" (if truly TBD, give a target range) |
| **Metrics** | Measurable targets with baselines | Vanity metrics or unmeasurable goals |
| **Investment** | Specific dollar amounts and approval status | Hiding the cost. Be upfront. |
| **Ask** | One clear, specific request | Multiple asks buried in text |

## Gotchas

- A one-pager is ONE PAGE. If it doesn't fit, you're including too much detail. Move details to an appendix.
- The "Ask" section is the most important. If you don't know what you're asking for, you don't need a one-pager — you need a conversation.
- Use the "Why" section to connect to goals your audience cares about. Your director cares about pipeline. Your VP cares about competitive positioning. The CVP cares about portfolio impact.
- "Green/Yellow/Red" status should match reality, not optimism. Leaders respect honest Yellow more than fake Green.
- Always include metrics, even if the program hasn't started. Show what you'll MEASURE, not just what you'll DO.
- If your one-pager is for a decision: make the decision easy. Include your recommendation, the cost, and the deadline.
